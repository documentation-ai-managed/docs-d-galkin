---

## Overview

Projects help you organize your work in Di.G.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Projects">
    From the left menu, click "Projects".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create new">
    Click "Add Project" at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill details">
    Enter a project name and optionally a description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Create" to add your new project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
