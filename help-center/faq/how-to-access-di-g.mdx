## Overview

Di.G is available via web and mobile app.

## Steps to Access

1. Visit `https://app.digalkin.com`
2. Click "Sign Up" to create an account
3. Confirm your email
4. Log in with your credentials

## Mobile Access

Download the Di.G app from the App Store or Play Market, then log in with your account.
